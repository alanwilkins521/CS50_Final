Welcome to my online Farkle app!

You will try your luck against the CPU

Game Rules:

  • Roll 6 die, choose which scoring die to keep, according to this scoring:
    • 5 = 50 points
    • 1 = 100 points
    • 3 of a kind of 2-6: 100 x that number (e.g. 3 x 3's is 300 points)
    • 3 of a kind of 1's: 1,000 pionts
    • 4 of a kind: 1,000 points
    • 5 of a kind: 2,000 points
    • 6 of a kind: 3,000 points
    • Straight (1-6): 1,500 points
    • 3 pairs: 1,500 points
    • 4 of a kind + pair: 1,500 points
    • 2 x 3 of a kind: 2,500 points
  • Any roll that has at least one scoring die may be improved upon. Set aside the scoring die that you'd like to keep, and reroll the others
  • If all available die are rolled as scoring die, you may pick them all up and keep rolling!
  • To get your score banked for the first time, your roll must score at least 1,000 points
  • If you do not score at least 1,000 points, and/or you do not roll any scoring die, your score for that turn is 0
  • Once you have at least 1,000 points, you may build off the score of the previous players roll!
  • First player to 10,000 points wins!
